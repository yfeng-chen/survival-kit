My favorite ice cream

Ice cream is a frozen dessert usually made from dairy products, such as milk and cream, and often combined with fruits or other ingredients and flavors.
Most varieties contain sugar, although some are made with other sweeteners. In some cases, artificial flavorings and colorings are used in addition to the natural ingredients.
I love ice cream because it’s cold, there are so many flavors, and it’s sweet! There is so much ice cream out there…waiting…waiting for you to come and taste those reasons!
